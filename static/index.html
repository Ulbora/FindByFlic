Find FFL by Zip

Find FFL by Zip is a shopping cart solution for finding a local FFL while in the checkout process.

You still need to validate the FFL as required by the ATF, but Find FFL by Zip helps to speed up the process.

Find FFL by Zip integrates easily into most shopping carts with either iframes or REST services.
